Forest Reserve La yeguada

La Yeguada Forest Reserve is one of the main tourist attractions in the province of Veraguas, it is very popular, since many people come to camp around it, because it has beautiful landscapes and a cool climate. La Yeguada is a very nice place to spend time with the family. Offers two places to camp, where the first is on the shore, and another site where, besides having access to a waterfall, there are also toilets and showers.
