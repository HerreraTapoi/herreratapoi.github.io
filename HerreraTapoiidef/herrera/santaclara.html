Santa Clara Beach

Panama is paradise for those who love sun, sand, waves and dreamy landscapes. And one of those landscapes is Santa Clara Beach, which, despite not being one of the most popular in the country, has the same characteristics. White sand that does not stick or fly, crystal clear aquamarine waters, corals and countless marine species.
